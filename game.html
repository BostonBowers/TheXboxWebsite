Halo

Halo is one of, if not the most popular xbox exclusive game. This game has you fighting hostile aliens with a diverse assortment of weapons and gear. The game attracted many followers for its fun combat and its great story. This game has left a mark on so many childhoods that many call it the greatest game of all time. The fierce and brave Master Chief went down as one of the greatest video game characters of its time. This game has won the hearts of many and will forever be loved by the community of gammers.

This game has stunning graphics and amazing replayability. Some darring pirates even claimed to see the infamous megaladon and the terrifying kraken!
